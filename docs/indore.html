Indore GAMES

Chess

Chess is a board game for two players. It is sometimes called international chess or Western chess to distinguish it from related games such as xiangqi and shogi. Chess is an abstract strategy game that involves no hidden information and no elements of chance.

LUDO

Ludo is a strategy board game for two to four players, in which the players race their four tokens from start to finish according to the rolls of a single die. Like other cross and circle games, Ludo is derived from the Indian game Pachisi.

UNO

UNO is a multi-player card game in which the objective is to be the first player to get rid of all the cards in their hand. Each player is dealt 7 cards and players take turn drawing cards from the deck.
